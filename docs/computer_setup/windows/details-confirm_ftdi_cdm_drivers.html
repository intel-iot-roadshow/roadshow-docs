Confirm installation of FTDI serial drivers

Look for “USB Serial Port” in Device Manager under “Ports (COM & LPT)” after plugging in the UART/serial USB cable.

  1. Power the Intel® Edison via the device mode micro-USB port and/or via the power barrel connector. or

  2. Connect a micro-USB cable to the UART/serial micro-USB port of the Intel® Edison expansion board, and the other end to your computer. Refer to UART/serial micro-USB cable for more detailed cable connection information.

If you see “USB Serial Port” show up in Device Manager under “Ports (COM & LPT)”, the drivers have been successfully installed.
